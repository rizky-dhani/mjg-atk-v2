# Initial Concept
MJG ATK Management System: A comprehensive inventory management solution for office stationery (ATK) built with Laravel and Filament v4, enabling efficient supply management across multiple divisions with robust approval workflows.

# Product Vision
To provide a transparent, efficient, and accountable ecosystem for managing organizational resources, ensuring that every piece of stationery is accounted for and optimized for organizational needs.

# Target Users
*   **Staff:** For requesting necessary supplies for daily operations.
*   **Heads of Division:** For reviewing and managing division-specific inventory needs.
*   **Admins:** For system-wide inventory and workflow management.
*   **Management & Executives:** For organization-wide supply cost analytics and strategic decision-making.

# Business Goals
*   **Cost Reduction:** Identifying and eliminating wasteful spending through precise usage tracking and data-driven insights.
*   **Operational Efficiency:** Streamlining manual processes to reduce administrative overhead for all staff levels.
*   **Accountability:** Maintaining a rigorous audit trail of inventory movements and approvals to ensure integrity.

# Key Features
*   **Dynamic Inventory Tracking:** Real-time visibility into stock levels across all organizational divisions.
*   **Direct Stock Distribution:** Ability for central administration (GA) to directly transfer or bulk-distribute items from general "Floating Stock" to specific divisions.
*   **Formal Stock Requests:** Standardized request workflow for divisions to request items from central "Floating Stock", fully integrated with the multi-step approval system.
*   **Multi-Step Approval Workflows:** Flexible, configurable logic that adapts to different request types and organizational hierarchies.
*   **Intelligent Notifications:** Real-time alerts (email/system) to minimize bottlenecks in the approval chain.
*   **Mobile-First Approval Experience:** Ensuring decision-makers can approve or reject requests seamlessly from any device.
*   **Advanced Analytics:** Comprehensive reporting for management to monitor costs and consumption patterns.
